About me

I received the B.S. degree from University of Science and Technology of China, in 2022, and is now persuing for the master's degree. I am currently a postgraduate student with the Key Laboratory of Wireless-Optical Communications, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and School of Information Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China. I am also a visiting student in School of Science and Engineering and Future Network of Intelligence Institute (FNii), The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen.

My main research interests include 6G Wireless Communication, Integrated Sensing and Communication (ISAC), Simultaneous Wireless Communication and Power Transfer (SWIPT), and Energy Efficient Communication.
